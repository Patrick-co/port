Rios Landscaping - Coming Soon Page

Web Design


Rios Landscaping & Services is a local landscaping company focused on providing reliable lawn care and property maintenance for both residential and commercial properties. The goal of the business is to help clients maintain clean, healthy, and great-looking outdoor spaces they can be proud of.

The “Coming Soon” page was built as a first step in establishing an online presence while the full website is still in progress. Its purpose is to give potential customers an immediate place to learn about the company, understand the services offered, and easily get in touch—helping Rios Landscaping start building trust and visibility from day one.

View: Rios Landscaping Countdown





Logo Design


The Rios Landscaping & Services logo represents professionalism, reliability, and quality outdoor care. Its clean, bold design makes it easy to recognize and versatile across the website, print materials, and vehicles, while reflecting the company’s connection to nature and landscaping.

The logo was created by my graphic designer friend, Thang, with the brand’s vision in mind. He focused on a modern, approachable look that feels trustworthy and works well across all platforms as the business grows.
